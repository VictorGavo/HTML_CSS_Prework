My Why

On some level we are all part of the same tribe. When you look at the vastness of space and how empty it is, even with all our faults... The human race is worth preserving. Just like you, I beat out a lot of competition to get here, and I ain't keen on spending it all willy-nilly. I'm going to leave things better than when I found them. I'm going to uplift those around me, encourage them to shoot their shot, because as far as I'm aware, we only experience what this universe has to offer one time. Would be cool to be wrong, but I think its better to behave as if I'm right, and learn as I go. I want to learn everything I can, so that I can teach the next batch and give them the best chance I can of pushing us farther. Who's going to be the one to solve Alzheimers? Cancer? You think human development is on an exponential curve? What happens as less of us have to spend our mental resources on that first tier of Maslow's hierarchy? That's the world I want to live in. That's my why.

The Who

My name is Victor Gavojdea, and I'd love to be your friend! I'm first generation American, my parents came from Romania to have a better life, they made huge sacrifices and took the biggest risks just for the opportunity. Family is a big part of who I am, and I am blessed to have a big family full of amazing examples, not just from my parents, I am the youngest of 7 with all but 2 of us being adopted* into the family.
